top 10

01

Stress Free Year No Tests and No Need to Find a Place to Go for Shabbat or Chag because Tiferet is ALWAYS open And if you have any problems - all the administrators live very close by. And don’t forget - at Tiferet all the learning is Torah Lishma. No tests. Just high level learning to grow intellectually, spiritually and emotionally.

02

Easiest Adjustment within just the first week of Tiferet, you have been to at least 4 teachers homes for meals, your teachers know your name and you get to know their families. Very quickly students refer to Tiferet as home. Spending Shabbat, Yom Tov and Purim Seudot and Vaad’s in your teachers homes creates the feeling that you are not only in a school - you’re part of a family.

Learning

Learning Torah on a high level not only challenges your mind. It also elevates your soul. With text of classical sources in every Shiur, you watch the text come to life as our Dream Team of teachers (as one high school principle called it) delve inside. The variety of classes are dizzying. In fact, the biggest complaint we receive from students is that it’s so hard to choose from all the options. They want to take every single one! But don’t fret. You can always make a Chavruta with your teachers on any topic you wish. On average each students has at least 5 Chavruta’s with teachers and complete many Sefarim.
