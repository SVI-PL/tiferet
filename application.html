Learning

Learning Torah on a high level not only challenges your mind. It also elevates your soul. With text of classical sources in every Shiur, you watch the text come to life as our Dream Team of teachers (as one high school principle called it) delve inside. The variety of classes are dizzying. In fact, the biggest complaint we receive from students is that it’s so hard to choose from all the options. They want to take every single one! But don’t fret. You can always make a Chavruta with your teachers on any topic you wish. On average each students has at least 5 Chavruta’s with teachers and complete many Sefarim.

If you are interested in applying to Tiferet, you must submit the following at the above website:

  • Complete the online Joint Application Form at the above website
  • Download and complete the 2-page medical form at the above website
  • Two letters of recommendation from teachers or principals, done at the above website
  • A recent passport photo
  • The most recent transcript from your school, uploaded to the above website
  • Essay on one of the suggested topics, uploaded to the above website
  • Application fee of $100, or equivalent, paid by credit card when you submit the application on-line
more info

The application form must be completed and submitted on-line but if there are any parts of the application that you will NOT send via the internet, please mail it or fax it to: American Friends of Tiferet 843 Jefferson Street Woodmere, NY 11598 Fax: (718) 338-1250 We will begin to process your application once all of the above has been received. If you have any questions, please call us at our New York office (718-253-0230).
